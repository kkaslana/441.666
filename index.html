Teaching concept

1. Pay attention to the progress and development of students. Teachers are required to have the consciousness of "object". Teachers must establish the dominant position of students and establish the idea of "everything for the development of students".

2. Pay attention to teaching efficiency and require teachers to have the concept of time and efficiency. Teaching effectiveness does not depend on how much content teachers teach.

3. Focus on testability and quantification. For example, the teaching objectives should be as clear and specific as possible in order to test the work efficiency of teachers.

Teaching purpose

1. Let students understand the course and master the course skills

2. Let students have fun in class and learn knowledge happily
